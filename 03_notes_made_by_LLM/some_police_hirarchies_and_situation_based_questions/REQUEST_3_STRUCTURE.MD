
### **GUIDE 3: BIHAR ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICE HIERARCHY**

**(The Frame of Steel - इस्पात का ढांचा)**

Here is the strategic structure for the third guide. This is more complex than the police guide because Administration has **two faces**: Field and Secretariat.

#### **MODULE 1: THE DUAL IDENTITY (दो दुनिया)**

**Theme:** *Where do you actually work?*

* **The Concept:** Explain that a BAS officer is not just a "Field Officer." He is a **Generalist**.
* **World 1 (Field):** Direct public dealing (SDM, ADM).
* **World 2 (Secretariat):** Policy making in Patna (Under Secretary, Deputy Secretary).


* **The Entry Point:** You enter as **Senior Deputy Collector (SDC)**. Explain what this designation means vs. SDM.

#### **MODULE 2: THE LADDER OF POWER (पद और प्रमोशन)**

**Theme:** *From Block to Mantralaya – The Path.*

* **Level 1: The Foundation (Block Level - Your Subordinates):**
* BDO (Block Development Officer) & CO (Circle Officer).
* *Key Insight:* They are "Group B" officers; you are "Group A." You inspect them.


* **Level 2: The Middle Management (Sub-Division & District - Your Playground):**
* **SDM/SDO (You):** The King of the Sub-division.
* **ADM (Additional District Magistrate):** The next promotion. The "Little DM."
* **DDC (Deputy Development Commissioner):** The Development Head (IAS/Senior BAS).
* **DM (District Magistrate):** The Boss (IAS).


* **Level 3: The Policy Heads (Secretariat/Commissionary):**
* Commissioner (Divisional Head).
* Secretariat Ranks: Under Secretary  Deputy Secretary  Joint Secretary.



#### **MODULE 3: THE COMMAND CHAIN (रिपोर्टिंग और प्रोटोकॉल)**

**Theme:** *Who writes your CR (Confidential Report)?*

* **The Reporting Matrix:**
* **BDO  SDO (You)  DM.**
* *Real Life:* Why the SDO is the "eyes" of the DM.


* **The "Protocol" Trap:**
* Relationship with the **SP**: You are equal in rank, but have different domains.
* Relationship with **Politicians (MPs/MLAs):** Protocol dictates "Courtesy," not "Subservience."
